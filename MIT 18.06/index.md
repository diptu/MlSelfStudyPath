# Study Plan: MIT 18.06 Linear Algebra (Prof. Gilbert Strang)

This repository contains a 6-week intensive study plan to master Linear Algebra, the foundational mathematics behind modern Machine Learning, Graphics, and Engineering.

## 📅 Weekly Schedule

### Week 1: Solving Ax = b
**Goal:** Master matrix elimination and the mechanics of solving linear systems.
- **Lectures:** 1 (Geometry of Linear Equations), 2 (Elimination), 3 (Matrix Multiplication/Inverses), 4 (LU Factorization), 5 (Transposes/Permutations).
- **Core Concepts:** Gaussian Elimination, Matrix Inversion, Row/Column views of multiplication.

### Week 2: The Four Fundamental Subspaces
**Goal:** Move beyond mechanics to the "Big Picture" of vector spaces.
- **Lectures:** 6 (Vector Spaces), 7 (Nullspace), 8 (Solving Ax=b), 9 (Independence/Basis), 10 (The Four Subspaces).
- **Core Concepts:** Column space, Nullspace, Rank, Dimension, Basis.

### Week 3: Orthogonality & Least Squares
**Goal:** Understand projections and how to solve "unsolvable" systems (data fitting).
- **Lectures:** 14 (Orthogonality), 15 (Projections), 16 (Least Squares), 17 (Orthogonal Matrices/Gram-Schmidt).
- **Core Concepts:** Projection matrices, Normal equations, A = QR decomposition.

### Week 4: Determinants & Eigen-Analysis
**Goal:** Understand the "DNA" of a matrix—its eigenvalues and eigenvectors.
- **Lectures:** 18 (Determinant Properties), 19 (Formulas), 21 (Eigenvalues/Vectors), 22 (Diagonalization).
- **Core Concepts:** Det(A), Diagonalization (A = SΛS⁻¹), Powers of A.

### Week 5: Singular Value Decomposition (SVD)
**Goal:** Master the single most important decomposition in data science and image compression.
- **Lectures:** 25 (Symmetric Matrices), 26 (Complex Matrices), 27 (Positive Definite Matrices), 29 (SVD).
- **Core Concepts:** Spectral Theorem, Principal Component Analysis (PCA) foundations, SVD (A = UΣVᵀ).

### Week 6: Linear Transformations & Advanced Topics
**Goal:** Connect abstract transformations to matrix operations.
- **Lectures:** 30 (Linear Transformations), 31 (Change of Basis), 32 (Image Compression), 33 (Left/Right Inverses).
- **Core Concepts:** Basis change, Pseudoinverses, Applications in Engineering.
